This couldn't be any cooler:

It's all in the controls!

Each d9 system leverages our unique SmartPDU design to deliver unparalleled convenience, customisation and intelligence to the grow lamp market.

Real time clock (with battery).

The D9 PDU always knows what time it is - and what part of the cycle it should be executing. It will always deliver the correct output for any given time of the day, even after a power loss.

Self-contained and controlled.

Each PDU ships with all of our standard dynamic and static profiles; and the ability to create and store as many of your own as you like! Switching between profiles at the touch of a button.

All you need is love (and outlets).

d9NX3 systems only need to be plugged into normal wall power. There is no need for external switches, dimmers, or timers or complex splice installations. No electrician necessary!

Open Source IOT Ready.

The SmartPDU is managed via a WiFi+HTML interface. Further updates will enable IoT and Mesh support. The system is fully open source and we welcome customisations and unique solutions from our community!
