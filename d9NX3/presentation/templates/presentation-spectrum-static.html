Included static profiles:

Static profiles are very useful for harvesting, interacting and experimenting with your crops. For construction and general lighting purposes. And, when using D9NX3 with external timers.


This profile sets D9NX3 to 66% Blue+Red and 33% Green, always on.

This is similar to a standard grow lamp's spectrum output when using HPS or "Blue Veg" LEDs.

This mode is particularly well suited to stimulate a reasonable amount of Vegetative growth while not completely tilting the balance towards it, or, to germinate new seedlings.
This profile sets D9NX3 to 75% Blue+Red and 33% Green, always on.

With even less Green and more Blue+Red, this mode simulates an HPS light while far outperforming it on uMol/Watt, Growth per day and thermal limits.

This mode is particularly well suited to boost young plants into a very rapid vegetative growth cycle - it will result in taller, skinnier plants than those grown with more Green.
This profile sets D9NX3 to 50% Blue and 50% Red, always on.

This is the closest mode to a "standard" grow lamp: The Green channel is disabled and D9NX3 only outputs a full mixture of Blue and Red.

This mode boosts both plant metabolisms (Vegetative and Blooming) once plants are established and of good size.
This profile sets D9NX3 to White generated by equal amounts of R,G,B.

This mode easily allows Growers to interact with their crops: it provides a comfortable shade of white for our eyes.

"Harvest" Mode is the closest mode to a standard, white LED light.

Each of these three profiles sets the output to any one colour.

This mode lets you experiment with your plants and your growing conditions, and is is provided as a starting point for you to measure and adjust DLI per colour.

And you can always throw parties after the harvests you will have with D9NX3 afterwards
