Included static profiles:

Static profiles are very useful for harvesting, interacting and experimenting with your crops. For construction and general lighting purposes. And, when using D9NX3 with external timers.


Blue:Red:Green Ratios
output a fixed setpoint with a specific ratio of (Blue:Red)+Green Light.

  • 50% Blue+Red; 50% Green
  • 66% Blue+Red; 33% Green
  • 75% Blue+Red; 25% Green
  • 90% Blue+Red; 10% Green

Simulates many different types of LPS and HPS/Halide traditional lighting system's output.

Blue:Red Ratios
output a fixed setpoint with a specific ratio of Blue:Red.

  • 50% Blue 50% Red
  • 66% Blue 33% Red
  • 75% Blue 25% Red
  • 90% Blue 10% Red

Ultra high efficiency growing spectrums that specifically target the vegetative and flowering/blooming responses in plants.

Harvest
full spectrum white light

This mode sets the d9NX3 fixture to a low-power mixture of B:G:R that results in full-spectrum white light; specifically designed for harvesting and interacting with your plants. This cycle does not disturb photosynthesis in the middle of a growth cycle.

Whenever you need to interact with your plants; you need to be able to see clearly!

Static Output (R,G,B)
output full power in a single colour: red,green,blue.

These profiles set the fixture to full-power output in one specific spectrum: Blue, Green, or Red. For the specific cases where you need no blooming photosynthesis, you can use pure blue output. In case that you need to arrest vegetative growth and induce blooming, use static red.

Full power to any colour is extremely bright! Blue and Red will produce extremely high uMol.
