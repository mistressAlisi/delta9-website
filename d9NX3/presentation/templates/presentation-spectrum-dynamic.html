This profile simulates a summer day with twelve hours of irradiation.

Delta9 starts every day by supplying reds and oranges; adding in greens and finally blues as it approaches midday.

Towards the end of the cycle, first blues fade out, then greens, and finally reds and deep reds to simulate a natural sundown.

Plants that grow under these conditions are subject to less stress and far more robust metabolic rates than plants grown under static light.
