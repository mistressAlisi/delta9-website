Included dynamic profiles:

Dynamic profiles bring the rythms of nature indoors: They replicate the natural patterns of light that power outdoor agriculture (without that pesky weather getting in the way.)


12/16H Summer Profile
simulate a summer day with 12/16 hours of irradiation

Delta9 starts every day by supplying reds and oranges; adding in greens and finally blues close to noon. Towards the end of the cycle, first blues fade out, then greens, and finally reds and deep reds to simulate a natural sundown.

Plants that grow under these conditions are subject to less stress and far more robust metabolic rates than plants grown under static light.

Veg Booster
stimulate the growth of vegetation and plant mass; taller and skinnier plants.

This profile decreases the 16h cycle's peak red output by 30%, simulating late spring and summer days. The decrease in red light in relation to the blue light, along with long days stimulates plants to grow vegetative mass - growing taller and larger.

This cycle is particularily well suited to stimulate vegetative growth.

12H Fall Day
simulate a fall day with 12 hours of irradiation

Delta9 starts every day by supplying reds and oranges; adding in greens and finally blues close to noon. Towards the end of the cycle, first blues fade out, then greens, and finally reds and deep reds to simulate a natural sundown. This profile tilts the Summer spectrum towards the dominant red ratio, stimulating vegetation

This profile helps transition plants grown under summer-like sunlight into full-on harvest mode naturally, as it happens outdoors.

Flower Power
stimulate flowering and blooming and the growth of thicker, smaller plants.

This profile decreases the 12h Fall cycle's blue output peak by 30%, simulating fall and early winter days. The decrease in blue light in relation to red light stimulates plants to grow flowers, fruits and vegetables, and to grow thicker.

This cycle is extremely useful to induce flowering or the gestation of fruits and veggies.
