The SmartPDU sits at the heart of the NX3 design: It controls the LED output for each channel, the fans, and the power levels. Taking it one level further it includes a smart, WiFi and Bluetooth enabled digital front end for easy provisioning and use of our unique features.

Pure digital magic!

Control all the features of your D9NX3 fixture using a single, HTML control interface from any device on your network. No more software required!

No more Clocks or Timers.

With its integrated clock, each NX3 SmartPDU is fully manageable on a schedule out of the box without the need for external mechanical timers (Or Electricians installing it)
