It begins with a well rounded spectrum...

Delta9 targets four key frequencies of light to optimally support photosynthesis and plant growth:

Deep Blue

Max: 1,941μMol

Deep blue stimulates vegetative growth and powers the Clorophyll A/B Photosynthesis Metabolism - plants need this frequency to germinate and grow.

Deep Red

Max: 1,497μMol

Deep Red stimulates blooming growth and powers the Carotenes Photosynthesis Metabolism - plants need it to blossom and fruit.


Green

Max: 1,227μMol

Green is somewhat reflected back by plants (that's why they appear green!) But it controls critical signals to plant metabolism controlling growth and morphology.

Far Red

Max: 135μMol

The shadow/far-red response helps the plant transition from metabolic states (darkness/light) - and grow in the right direction. Healthy plants depend on the Far-Red response.
