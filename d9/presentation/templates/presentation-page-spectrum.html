This profile simulates a summer day with twelve hours of irradiation.

Delta9 starts every day by supplying reds and oranges; adding in greens and finally blues as it approaches midday.

Towards the end of the cycle, first blues fade out, then greens, and finally reds and deep reds to simulate a natural sundown.

Plants that grow under these conditions are subject to less stress and far more robust metabolic rates than plants grown under static light.

It's all in the customisation!

The delta9 PDU has 10 configuration slots in addition to the 4 standard profiles.

Each slot can be configured in 15 minute increments per channel, using advantage of the built in 24/7 RTC clock.

Or perhaps you need a specific interface to your super-custom arduino-powered grow data hub: The PDU's interfaces are open source.

At launch, the firmware implements an http profile editor/selector. Future IOT and Software deployments are planned and will be delivered as software updates.
